Education & Background

Who I Am &
Where I Come From

I'm a multidisciplinary artist from Iran. Graphic design, filmmaking, 3D animation, photography, video editing: five years of building across all of it, with one consistent thread. Every frame, every cut, every composition is a decision about what to say and how to say it.

I studied drama at the Fine Arts School of Hamedan — not the obvious background for a designer, but it's where I learned to think about an audience: what they feel, not just what they see. That instinct shapes everything I make.

Currently admitted to NHL Stenden University (Communication & Multimedia Design) in the Netherlands, working toward becoming an art director and creative lead.
